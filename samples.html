Realstate Bidding DAPP

A DAPP using Solidity in order to publish your real state as ERC-721 token on Ethereum. I used truffle to develope the backedn which is in solidity. For connecting the smart contract on backend, I used ReactJs to develop a frontend. For eas of use and show th DAPP quicker, there is no authentication and all users are listed as different tabs on left panel of the DAPP. Users can mint their realtstate as ERC-721 token and later assign a realtor to handle its marketing and bidding process. Highest bid then later will be announced as winner by realtor and change of ownership occures.
